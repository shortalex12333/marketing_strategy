## 9 · Anti-pattern guardrails (re-stated for any future session reading this file)

1. **No "2am" / "3am" / "at midnight" hooks.** Time-of-day framing is retired per skill §3.3 — projecting onto reader. Use scenario language (mid-rotation arrival, surveyor visit, class audit) instead.

3. **No engagement bait.** No `DM "PILOT"` / `Comment "X"` / `Tag a friend`. Caption closes on URL or nothing.
